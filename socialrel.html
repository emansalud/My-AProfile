How We Connect

  • Shared Growth: Supporting each other’s goals and having their back as they got mine. Friendship is all about how people connect with each other with unconditional support and help.
  • Authenticity: Building relationships based on honesty and genuine care. We do not tolerate inappropriate things, instead, we criticize each other to make our mistakes a lesson
  • Communication: Staying connected regardless of distance or busy schedules. We always find ways how we can bond even though we are busy, whether it's an online games or hangout, we never fail to make each other laugh as we get together.
